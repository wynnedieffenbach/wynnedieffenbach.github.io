Let's Dress up!
Welcome!
We believe that all types of games should be inclusive, regardless of gender or race. Inspired by traditional online Barbie dress up games, we created an interactive game that strives to include all players.
How to Play
It's simple! First, click the doll you want to dress up below. Next, click on the whatever item of clothing you want them to wear. Using your arrow keys, move the piece of clothing to fit the doll. Press the 'A' button to add more clothes. Once you've finished, just click done!
