The Hedley Farms Marathon
About my Project
I decided to create this infographic for my final project in Data Visualization. In October, I ran a marathon with a few members of my family. However, because of COVID, we were unable to have a traditional, high-energy, congratulatory finish. I wanted to create something that could take the place of that. I created this infograph to be a way to remember the run, as well as an opportunity to visualize the data of the run months after the fact. My visualization features a map of the route we ran along in my hometown.
