Our Mission

We believe that in order for the earth to take care of us, we need to take care of the earth. Founded in 2008 by Mable J. Matthews, Promise Plant Products creates skincare that takes a lesson from Mother Nature, all while giving right back. We've partnered directly with local farmers in Malaysia to make sure our products contain the most ethically sourced, environmentally friendly, and gentle yet effective ingredients.

Meet Our Founder

Mable J. Matthews is the CEO and Founder of Promise Plant Products. Born in Sweden, she grew up living and learning in nature. After graduating from Sweden's top Ecological Studies program, she decided to make a change. She began developing skincare products with her passion for the environment at the core.
